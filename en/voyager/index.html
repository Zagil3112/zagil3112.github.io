About Us

Voyager is a research group with aeronautical and astronautical approaches created by a group of students from group of students of Aerospace Engineering at the University of Antioquia in 2017, initially, as a group of scientific dissemination. Currently, dedicated to the design and construction of systems for aerospace vehicles, such as: sensing systems, communications, structure, image processing, among others.

With experience and outstanding participation in national competitions, in which we have obtained the first places with the design and construction of small satellites, zagi wings, rovers and others. The research group, is constantly looking for new challenges in which we test our skills and abilities of ingenuity, creativity and problem solving, where we can contribute to society and build a better world.
